Witch City Experiences is a Salem, Massachusetts company offering immersive ways to experience the history and craft of America's most famous Witch City. Through Witch City Walking Tours and Witch City Broom Co., visitors can explore Salem's historic, haunted streets on a guided tour — or visit the Broom Co. shop at 246 Essex Street to craft and take home their own keepsake broom in a hands-on workshop.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Witch City Experiences?

Witch City Experiences is a Salem, Massachusetts company and the parent of two local brands: Witch City Walking Tours and Witch City Broom Co. Together they offer guided historic walking tours and hands-on broom-making in the heart of Salem.

Where is Witch City Experiences located?

Witch City Experiences and its brands are based in Salem, Massachusetts.

What experiences do you offer?

Two: guided historic walking tours of Salem through Witch City Walking Tours, and broom-making through Witch City Broom Co. — which also runs a full brick-and-mortar retail shop at 246 Essex St, Salem, MA, offering hands-on workshops and take-home craft kits.

Where is the Witch City Broom Co. shop located?

Witch City Broom Co. is a brick-and-mortar shop at 246 Essex St, Salem, MA 01970, in downtown Salem.

How do I book a walking tour or a broom-making workshop?

Book directly with each brand — visit witchcitywalkingtours.com for walking tours and witchcitybroomco.com for broom-making, or stop by the Broom Co. shop at 246 Essex St, Salem.
